Private files folder
====================

Files in this folder and its subfolders are accessible by anyone, if its name
is known.

- Please use unpredictable filenames or subfolder names to prevent unauthorized
  accesses.

- Don't use special characters, symbols or space in filenames and in subfolder
  names.

- Share the link in the following formats:

    https://your-host-address/private/filename
    https://your-host-address/private/subfolder/filename
